ball.  How much does the ball cost?  \textbf{S1-lure}: \$0.10.
\textbf{S2-correct}: \$0.05.  Our novel variants replace the surface features
(e.g., ``A notebook and a pen cost \$2.20\ldots'') while preserving the
mathematical structure.
